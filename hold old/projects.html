99


This game started as a failed attempt to make snake, when I had first started playing around with coding in high school. The result was an odd version of snake where instead of it moving based on time and growing the snake, the field slowly fills with obstacles, while you manually move the character. The current state of the game has no way to win, but I plan on making the win condition getting a score of 99, hence the title. The balancing of the game still needs some work to make 99 feasible, but in the meantime try playing for yourself, and get a new high score!
