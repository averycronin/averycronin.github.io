Meet Avery

Get to know me a bit better.

DoD SMART Scholar

I was selected as a recipient of the DoD SMART Scholarship, a program aimed to recruit workers for government positions. In exchange for working for a government facility after graduating, I receive funding for school, along with a stipend.

Brother of Theta Tau

Theta Tau is a professional engineering fraternity, which I rushed and pledged in Spring 2021. I have maintained an active brother status within the fraternity and enjoy the philanthropy, service, brotherhood and professional development aspects of the fraternity.

University of South Carolina Student

I am currently a Junior at the University of South Carolina pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science.
